Diabetics Foot Ulcres Classification Tool

Introduction

DFU identification and treatment processes have been problematic up to now. Existing clinical approaches to DFU treatment have lots of limitations due to high cost in diagnosis, treatment as well as it has a lengthy process of care of the ulcers. Also diagnosis of DFU is very crucial as it has to be done by an expert otherwise if the ulcer was not diagnosed or misdiagnosed, it can lead to an imputation of a limb of a patient. So the diagnosis part of DFU has become an emerging field in diabetic care.

This research

Aim of this research is to improve the existing dataset of DFUs. Generative Adversarial Networks have been used to increase the dataset size. This tool can be used to compare the classification accuracy of the Pre-augmentation classification model and Post-Augmentation classification model.
